Commemorative 30th new single "Yukuen" released! 2 coupling songs are also available for distribution!

ASIAN KUNG-FU GENERATION's 30th new single "Yukuen" will be released today on 2/8 (Wed.)! In addition, two songs, the coupling songs "Weather Report" and "Hisaka Downhill", have also started to be distributed on each distribution site.   ■ Released on 2/8 (Wed.) Single "Yukuen" [CD purchase] https://kmu.lnk.to/utgZeL [Ongoing delivery] https://kmu.lnk.to/Shukuen "Yukuen" is used as the January cool opening theme for the currently airing TV Tokyo animation "BORUTO NARUTO NEXT GENERATIONS", and was written specifically for this anime. The coupling song "Weather Report", which started distribution from today, was composed by Kita (g.vo) and Goto (vo.g), with Kita serving as the vocalist. "Nissaka Downhill" is a sequel to the album "Surf Bungaku Kamakura" released in 2008.   The bonus Blu-ray that comes with the first production limited edition of "Yukuen" will be released on July 23 from the tour "ASIAN KUNG-FU GENERATION Tour 2022 'Planet Folks'", which has been going on for about half a year since May last year. Part of the performance at the Hibiya Open Air Concert Hall held on Saturday is included. It was the first performance in 17 years at the Hibiya Open Air Concert Hall, and it was a memorial video.
